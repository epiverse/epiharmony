Instructions

  1. Open the epiHarmony app at http://localhost:3001/epiharmony/
  2. In the Configuration Panel, paste the schema URLs into the Source or Target schema fields
  3. For single schema: Use just the COVID-19 dataset URL
  4. For multiple schemas: Click "Add another URL" and add all 5 clinical trial URLs
  5. Click the "Load Source Schemas" or "Load Target Schemas" button
  6. Switch to the Vocabulary Mapper or Quality Control tabs to see the loaded schemas
